# Vitalyn Vision

## Mission

Build a Personal AI Health Companion that creates a lifelong health memory, helping users better understand their health and helping doctors make better-informed decisions.

## Problem

Healthcare is reactive. Doctors only see a short snapshot of a patient's health, while patients often forget important details.

## Vision

Humans forget. Doctors see snapshots. Vitalyn remembers the complete story.

## Core Principles

- AI assists, never replaces doctors.
- Privacy first.
- Longitudinal health memory is the core product.
- Simplicity over complexity.
- Preventive healthcare through continuous understanding.
