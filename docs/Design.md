# Vitalyn Design System

## Product Feel

Vitalyn should feel calm, premium, trustworthy, and practical. The UI should be inspired by Apple Health, Notion, Linear, and Calm without copying them directly.

## Principles

- Health information first; decoration second.
- Clear hierarchy and generous spacing.
- Accessible contrast in light and dark mode.
- Reusable components.
- No fear-based language.
- No clinical overclaiming.

## Web Requirements

- Responsive web app, not a marketing-only landing page.
- Calm, premium interface with light and dark modes.
- Dense but readable health timeline.
- Clear forms for account access and journal capture.
- Doctor mode should emphasize facts and safety boundaries.
- Reusable layout, button, panel, form, and event components.

## Flutter Requirements

- Material 3.
- Light and dark themes.
- Responsive layouts for phone and tablet.
- Smooth transitions.
- Feature-based folder structure.
- Reusable timeline, journal, summary, and form components.

## Core Screens

- Onboarding and account creation.
- Login.
- Daily journal.
- Timeline.
- Doctor mode.
- Reports and prescriptions.
- Settings and privacy controls.
